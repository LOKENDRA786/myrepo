New Year's Day

New Year's Day is celebrated on January 1st and marks the beginning of the year.

Independence Day

Independence Day, celebrated on July 4th, commemorates the Declaration of Independence in 1776.

Thanksgiving Day

Thanksgiving Day is celebrated on the fourth Thursday of November and is a time to give thanks.

Christmas Day

Christmas Day is celebrated on December 25th and is a major holiday honoring the birth of Jesus Christ.
